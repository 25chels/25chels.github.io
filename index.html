About Chelsea

Chelsea is a designer and artist driven by a profound love for rain and the quiet beauty it brings. As the founder of Chace In Rain Studio, she blends elements of nature, dreams, and emotion to create timeless, evocative designs. With a background rooted in artistic exploration, Chelsea’s approach is deeply intuitive yet deliberate, capturing fleeting moments of inspiration as if collecting raindrops.

“Let the rain speak, and let dreams shape the form.”

Rainwing

A delicate dance between rain and flight, “Whispers of Rainwing” captures the fleeting beauty of a butterfly gliding through a quiet downpour. Crafted from warm, golden brass, its wings are adorned with tiny, hand-hammered impressions — each mark echoing the gentle fall of raindrops on a still surface. “Every raindrop tells a story, and every wingbeat speaks of resilience.”

Shadows in Flux

Where light meets form, and shadow becomes a language of its own — “Shadows in Flux” explores the delicate interplay between concealment and revelation.

Experimental Architectural Model

Where light meets form, and shadow becomes a language of its own — “Shadows in Flux” explores the delicate interplay between concealment and revelation. This experimental architectural model investigates how veiled light (韜光) influences spatial perception and the fluidity of visibility within a built environment. “Light is not merely seen — it is felt, traced, and reimagined.”
